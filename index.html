The Hidden Truth About Why Most Reading Programs Fail

Countless online reading programs promise to help your child identify main ideas, remember key details, and boost comprehension. They showcase impressive strategies and engaging activities. But here's what they won't tell you: most students can't even read half the words on the page.

The Real Problem Behind Poor Reading Comprehension

When children struggle with comprehension, the issue isn't their thinking skills or attention span. The problem is far simpler and more fundamental: they can't decode the words in front of them.

You can't identify the main idea of a passage when you're stuck sounding out every third word. You can't remember key details when all your mental energy goes toward figuring out what the letters say. Poor comprehension isn't a comprehension problem—it's a word reading problem.
