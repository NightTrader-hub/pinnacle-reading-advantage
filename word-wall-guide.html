The Custom Word Wall is perhaps our most versatile system included in the Pinnacle Reading Advantage program. It offers you a tremendous amount of features that teachers and parents won't know how they got along without!

üìäCustomizable Chart Size

The chart is a grid featuring letters on the top row and numbers down the left column. It starts with six columns but can be adjusted sequentially down to two columns, allowing you to download up to 102 words at maximum capacity.

If you have a smaller word list, decrease the number of columns to increase word size for maximum visibility. Perfect for classroom displays and focused learning sessions!

üìùWord List Manager

The list manager allows you to name and save all your important word lists. Lists can vary widely from vocabulary lists, word wall lists, spelling words, rows from our Word Workbook, and more. Simply create the list on the chart, name it, and save it to our dropdown file.

üéÅ Pre-loaded Bonus

The dropdown list comes pre-loaded with 102 of the most important sight words! Use this list to populate your chart and start practicing immediately with all the amazing features.

This is an incredibly useful and time-saving feature. When the time is right, you can easily delete a list with the touch of a button.

üß† Smart Import Intelligence

Even if you copy an icon or any other non-word character, the Smart Import can discriminate and filter so your chart is left with only words in the boxes. This means you can easily copy any row or page from our Word Workbook and the words will appear on the grid instantly!

üéØPractice Selection

When it's time to practice your words in the Practice module, you can easily click a letter to select a column. This also greys out the entire chart except the chosen column. Likewise, you can choose any row by clicking a number.

Flexible Practice Options

You can select multiple rows/columns or even practice the whole chart! Open the Practice module directly below the chart and start practicing words from your selected areas.

Vocabulary Building Excellence

This is vocabulary building at its finest - combining reading skills with comprehension in one powerful tool.
